About this Recipe

An easy 6 ingredient side dish that is hold the perfect essence of a summer day. This one is such a crowd pleaser that it was served at Becky's Celebration of Life along with the recipe for those who wanted it.

While this recipe is simple in execution it's robust in flavor. And if you let this dish sit in the fridge over night it melds those flavors further and is 100% better the next day!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of Hellmans Only mayonaise
  • 1 1/2 lbs macaroni noodles
  • 1 can very small, tender sweet peas
  • 2 large crisp dill pickles
  • 1 tbsp celery seed
  • 1 red onion

Method

  1. Cook pasta until 3/4's done. Drain and cool with cold water.
  2. Combine everything, mix and store mac and cheese in fridge as macaroni continues cooking until finished

Notes

It's very important to only cook macaroni noodles 3/4's done. If you cook them more they will get soggy and mushy while in the fridge.
