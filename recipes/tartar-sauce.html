About this Recipe

This simple, 7 ingredient condiment will spruce up any dish you choose to dip in it. It's best with fish and seafood but trust me when I say you can use it on anything!

This recipe came from my wife Cynthia mother, Becky. It's been considered a "secret"recipe by the rest of the family and we routinley get family members asking us to share it. We've resisted doing so, until now...

Ingredients

  • 1 quart of Hellmans Only mayonaise
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 dill pickle, grated
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 1 tbsp chopped dill
  • 4 drops tobasco hot sauce

Method

  1. Combine all igredients and mix until combined.

Notes

Grandpa said when adding Tobasco sauce you have to say "in the name of the father, son, and holy ghost" for the sauce to be awesome!
